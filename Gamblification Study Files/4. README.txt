Short Description
----------------
Cross-sectional survey of individuals aged 16–25 residing in Spain (n = 1,000), fielded in June 2025. The dataset captures demographics, gaming practices, exposure to gamblified mechanics (e.g., loot boxes, randomized rewards, time-limited offers), spending bands, emotional responses, and perceptions among non-players. Data are shared as CSV with a complete codebook.
